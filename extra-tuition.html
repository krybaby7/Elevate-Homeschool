Our Weekly Program

Expert instruction in key subjects with personalized attention. Each 2-hour session is designed to build mastery and confidence.

📅 Sunday (3:30 to 5:50 pm)

English Proficiency

Improve your English proficiency with an experienced, native English teacher. These sessions cover topics such as reading comprehension techniques and approaches, vocabulary and grammar essentials for passing any high school English language exam, including the SAT.

📅 Monday (3:30 to 5:50 pm)

English Literature

Elevate your English ability with in-depth, advanced English literature study. These sessions will delve into literary devices, themes and analysis through poetry and prose in preparation for any high school literature exams including AP English Literature.

📅 Tuesday (3:30 to 5:50 pm)

Math Tuition

Develop your math capability with easy-to-understand Math tuition and practice. From fundamentals through to advanced concepts, we'll make sure you finally grasp all those topics you've been struggling with for any high school exams, including the SAT.

📅 Wednesday (3:30 to 5:50 pm)

Macroeconomics

Boost your knowledge with this advanced Macroeconomics course, suitable for preparation for AP Macroeconomics, or A-Level Economics exams. Explore basic concepts as well as finance and economic growth and trade to prepare you for a future in the business world.
